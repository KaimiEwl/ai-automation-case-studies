# AI Lead Qualification Pilot

## Business problem

Small teams lose time sorting inbound leads manually. Good buyers wait too long, weak-fit leads get too much attention, and the handoff to sales is inconsistent.

## Proposed workflow

1. Capture inbound lead data from a form, CRM, email or chat source.
2. Normalize the lead into a shared schema.
3. Score the lead by fit, urgency, budget signal and next-action clarity.
4. Generate a short sales brief with risk flags and recommended follow-up.
5. Route hot leads to a human and park lower-priority leads into a nurture lane.

## Result to show

The value is not "AI writes messages." The value is faster triage, cleaner context for a human operator and fewer missed high-intent leads.

## Public-safe scope

This public case study uses synthetic examples only. Client data, private lead lists and production prompts are intentionally excluded.

# Old Lead Reactivation Pilot

## Business problem

Many businesses have old leads, dormant CRM records or abandoned conversations that were never followed up properly. The challenge is deciding which contacts deserve attention and what message should be sent now.

## Proposed workflow

1. Import a stale lead list from CRM or spreadsheet.
2. Deduplicate and segment by source, last interaction, buyer type and likely intent.
3. Produce a reactivation reason and next-best action for each segment.
4. Draft short outreach variants that a human can approve.
5. Track replies, objections, meetings booked and disqualified contacts.

## Result to show

The workflow creates a practical revenue experiment: recover conversations from existing demand before buying new traffic.

## Public-safe scope

This public case study excludes real contacts, phone numbers, emails, company-specific research and raw campaign logs.
